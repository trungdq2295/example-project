Amazon Appflow
	Fully managed integration service that enables you to securely transfer data betweeen Software-as-a-Service (Saas) applications and AWS
	Sources: Saleforce, SAP, Zendesk, Slack, and ServiceNow
	Destination: AWs services likes Amazon S3, Amazon Redshift or non-AWS such as Snowflake and Saleforce
	Frequence: on a schedule, in response to event,s or on demand
	Data transformation capabilities like filtering and validation
	Encrypted over the public internet or privately over AWs PrivateLink
	don't spend time writing the integrations and leverage APIs immediately
